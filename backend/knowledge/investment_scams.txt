Investment scams promise unrealistic returns.

Common examples:
- Cryptocurrency scams.
- Forex scams.
- Fake stock tips.
- Ponzi schemes.

Warning signs:
- Guaranteed profits.
- Pressure to invest immediately.
- Secret investment opportunities.
- Celebrity endorsements without evidence.

Safety Tips:
- Verify investment firms.
- Never invest under pressure.
- Consult licensed financial advisors.
