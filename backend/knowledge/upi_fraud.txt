UPI fraud is one of the most common digital payment scams.

Fraudsters often pretend to be buyers, bank officials, or customer support executives.

Common warning signs include:
- Asking users to approve a "Collect Request."
- Requesting OTPs or UPI PINs.
- Sending fake payment screenshots.
- Claiming money has been transferred when it has not.

Safety Tips:
- Never share your UPI PIN.
- Verify payments inside your banking app.
- Reject unknown collect requests.
- Contact your bank if you suspect fraud.
