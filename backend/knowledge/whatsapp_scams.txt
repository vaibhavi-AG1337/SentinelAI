WhatsApp scams frequently involve impersonation.

Attackers may pretend to be:
- Family members
- Friends
- Bank officials
- Police officers
- Government agencies

Common signs include:
- Requests for urgent money transfers.
- Requests for OTPs.
- Unknown international numbers.
- Emotional or urgent language.

Safety Tips:
- Verify the sender through a phone call.
- Never share OTPs.
- Block and report suspicious accounts.
