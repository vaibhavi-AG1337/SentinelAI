Digital arrest scams involve fraudsters impersonating police, cybercrime officers, or government agencies.

They falsely accuse victims of crimes and threaten arrest unless money is transferred immediately.

Warning signs:
- Video calls in fake police uniforms.
- Demands for secrecy.
- Requests for bank transfers or cryptocurrency.
- Pressure to act immediately.

Safety Tips:
- Real law enforcement does not demand payment over calls.
- Verify official identities.
- Contact your local police station if unsure.
